Target Audience

The target audience for this site will be older teens, young adults, and adults who like to have the edge taken off checking the weather. As mentioned on the site plan page, we're going to make the most of what the weather is giving us. This can be appreciated by young and old alike.

Persona 1

Stanley is a 20 year old male who works as an assistant manager at Pizza Hut. He has graduated from high school. He is married and lives in an apartment. He loves to get outdoors and go fishing. He also loves walking his dog. Weather conditions are important to him, so he knows if he needs to bundle his dog up for walks or if it's good fishing weather. His primary device is an android phone.

Persona 2

Henrietta is a 34 year old female who is a homemaker. She is a busy mother who spends the day getting her children to an fro. She and her kids' superfan. She attends their soccer matches, cross country and track meets. Knowing the weather lets her know if her kids need a jacket for school. It's also vital for attending her kids' activties. Her primary devices are an android phone and an iPad.
