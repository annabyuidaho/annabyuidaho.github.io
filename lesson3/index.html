Site Plan

Site Title and Tagline

The site title is: Thermometer Buzz.

The site tagline is: What does the mercury say today?

Site Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to provide weather data. It would also be a place to have information on other weather news. This news would be important weather news like if it was snowing in Illinois. It would also be a place for users in Illinois to be able to participate and share what the weather is doing in their location.

The atmosphere for the site will be upbeat, playful, and yet relaxing. The weather "is what it is" so we might as well make the most of it and that's what we're going to do.
