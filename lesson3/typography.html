Typography

For simplicity and easy of use, two fonts were chosen for this site. Gelasio and Open Sans. Gelasio is the headline font and Open Sans is the paragraph font.

Gelasio was chosen as the headline font, because of it's letter Z. The serif font with the two Zs together really has the feel of movement.

ZZ

Open Sans was chosen as the paragraph font, because it is a nice clean font. It also has a lot of movement and has a nice fresh feel.

In additon to that, Open Sans is a sans-serif font and provides a nice contrast to the strong serif font that is Gelasio.

Where Fonts Will Be Applied

  • Header and Footer: Gelasio
  • Navigation: Open Sans
  • Headings and Subheadings: Gelasio
  • Paragraph Text: Open Sans
